# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a professional and respectful environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical merit.
- Provide evidence when making claims.
- Assume good intent.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks
- Harassment
- Derogatory language
- Public disclosure of security issues without coordination

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove
contributions or participants that violate this policy.
